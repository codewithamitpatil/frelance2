Hygiene Controller

Monitor & Control your Indoor Air Quality (IAQ)

All in one unit which measures & Controls critical parameters like CO2, PM2.5, Humidity, Temperature & VOC Did you know – ideal range for CO2 is 450 - 800ppm in closed room.

Usually CO2 levels found in office rooms/ meeting room / home (when occupied ) is in the range of 950 to 1500. A valid reason for your frequent fatigue and headaches.

Just plug a Hygiene Controller and monitor your IAQ parameter to find if your indoor environment is healthy.
